Favourite Sci-fi Books

A round up of the best science fiction books I read in 2022

The Space Between Worlds
by Micaiah Johnson

So good I read it twice! This novel features a queer relationship as well as space travel. Loved it!

Every Version of You
by Grace Chan

I discovered this one through IndyReads because they were hosting this as a book club.
Without that I may not have even heard of it and missed a really interesting read!

Battle of the Linguist Mages
by Scotto Moore

Now this one was a strange one! I hadn't read anything of his before this dark, adventurous audiobook was engaging for sure.

Hench
by Natalie Zina Walschots

Yet another 'new for me author' from whom I am eager for a sequel.
